Alaska Airlines is out front among the U.S. commercial airlines in testing and integrating APMS into its ongoing FOQA (Flight Operations Quality Assurance) program. I developed, installed, and trained the staff on the APMS Reports module, customized for the AS Flight/Events database. It is significant that this same body of end-user code is also installed at United Airlines with an entirely different format database. Although the two airlines use flight analysis software from two different commercial vendors, the APMS application has been adapted to perform in the same manner for both.

APMS is a NASA research project to statistically process, display, and analyze commercial airline flight data. This VB application takes data from MS Access, uses OLE automation to transmit the data to S-Plus for statistical processing, and implements an advanced graphics control to display the parameter traces and the statistical components.
