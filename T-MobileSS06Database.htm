This project was performed in conjunction with Advanced Technology Partners (Atlanta, GA) for T-Mobile corporate headquarters (Bellevue, WA). ATP conducted a nationwide technical survey of T-Mobile's 1186 point-of-sale retail stores.

I served as the West Coast and Mountain states project manager. I managed and coordinated the activities of 12 techs as they conducted the in-store equipment inventories from Colorado to Hawaii through the spring and summer. I hired and managed 4 data clerks to enter the data into the database nightly.

 

This division also served as the focal point for processing the daily data entry batches from all divisions around the country. Being on the west coast served as a geographic advantage in that the eastern, southern, and central divisions could assemble their data at the end of the workday and forward it to us so that we could consolidate it, perform QA on the data, and upload the data into the database.

 

I also wrote the Excel automation scripts for preprinting the survey forms with store information, developed the process for data entry used by data clerks in all the divisions, programmed Access VBA automation scripts to standardize the data fields, spec'd the format of the summary report provided back to the store managers, wrote an automated Excel comparison utility for identifying the changes made by the store managers in the returned reports, developed the update SQL queries for uploading the returned data from Excel back into Access. Finally I QA'ed, standardized, and prepared the final survey database for delivery to T-Mobile.
