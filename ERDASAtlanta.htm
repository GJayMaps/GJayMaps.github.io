Griff Jay

gcon@peak.org

As a company ERDAS was a pioneer in bringing to market the first commercially viable applications of raster GIS, image processing, and remote sensing.

For all ERDAS software between v2.0 to v7.5, I was the vector developer in the raster world. I was solely responsible for the map digitizing module, vector-to-raster conversion, and integration of USGS map projections. As the staff expanded, I was the technical lead on data conversion software, on the ESRI Arc/Info Live Link product, and on a major project for indexing aerial reconnaissance photos.

Also please see: ESRI Arc/Info Live Link, DIA Indexing, WB Map Digitizing, Alaska DGGS Geochemical Modeling System.
