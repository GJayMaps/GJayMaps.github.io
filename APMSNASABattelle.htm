Griff Jay

gcon@peak.org

APMS is a NASA research project to statistically process, display, and analyze commercial airline flight data. This VB application takes data from MS Access, uses OLE automation to transmit the data to S-Plus for statistical processing, and implements an advanced graphics control to display the parameter traces and the statistical components.

I was solely responsible for two modules of the APMS system. 1) The Atypicality module takes the flight data at each routine event and determines the most atypical flight amongst the selected dataset. 2) The Reports module automates the quarterly safety reporting requirements for two U.S. commercial airlines.

Please see: APMS United Airlines, APMS Alaska Airlines
