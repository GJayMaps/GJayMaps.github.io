On the front end and the back end of the Kuala Kencana GIS Needs Assessment project, which was the primary focus of the EISYS team in Indonesia, we were also asked to conduct a similar mini- Needs Assessment for enterprise GIS for the far flung activities of PT Freeport. The deliverable was a presentation to corporate management prior to departing on our return itinerary.

The multi-dimensional, multi-level aspects of the KK GIS (as described by Kuala Kencana GIS Needs Assessment) are obviously as much or more evident in this corporate situation where operational and administrative activities stretch from New Orleans to Jakarta to Irian Jaya. The project deliverable was met, and an effective presentation was prepared as requested, plus a requirements survey was prepared for the IS manager to use to continue the assessment. The need for an advanced tool for rapid project spec was highlighted and reinforced for the project team by this effort.

Please see the white paper Rapid Project Spec Using Object Technology for a more complete description of the requirements for an advanced rapid project spec product.
