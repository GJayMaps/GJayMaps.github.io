Aircraft Maintenance Officer

 

U. S. Air Force

Kelly Air Force Base

San Antonio, TX

 

1971-1975

 

Griff Jay

gcon@peak.org

Following graduation in 1971 and before returning to graduate school in 1975, I served as an Aircraft Maintenance Officer in the Air Force. I was Branch Chief in command of thirteen maintenance and electronics shops including 100 mechanics, technicians, and electronics specialists in support of all base-assigned and transient aircraft - from fighters to bombers to transport planes. We were also responsible for receiving all AirEvac planes with emergency burn patients being transported to the Army hospital's burn center. Our unit also was one of only four which handled the arrival flights for returning POWs.
