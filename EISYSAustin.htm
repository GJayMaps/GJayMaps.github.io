The collaborative EISYS projects include Kuala Kencana (Indonesia) GIS, ARCOView Field Data Collection, and SOM Global Grid. For details on those projects, please exercise the links. EISYS (purchased by 3DI Inc in 1999) is a consulting firm expert in remote sensing, image processing, and GIS.

A key aspect of the work with EISYS was my interaction with EISYS Ft.Collins' lead programmer Randy How via web-based, long-distance collaboration technologies. Randy & I programmed together almost daily, he in Colorado and me in Oregon by viewing screens simultaneously, editing common documents, sharing files, compiling programs, and even taking control of each other's mouse on occasion to demonstrate points and techniques.
